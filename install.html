Set Up Themes in 3 Minutes.

Download a .theme.css, move it into your themes folder, and enable it inside your client settings.

BetterDiscord Setup

  1. Open BetterDiscord settings inside Discord.
  2. Go to Themes and click Open Themes Folder.
  3. Place crimson.theme.css or purple.theme.css into that folder.
  4. Enable the toggle next to the theme name.
BetterDiscord Home

Vencord Setup

  1. Open Vencord settings.
  2. Go to Themes or QuickCSS and load your local theme file.
  3. Enable the selected theme.
  4. Reload Discord if changes do not appear immediately.
